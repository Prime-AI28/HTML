ATL Community Day

ATL Community Day aims to maximize the impact of Atal Tinkering Labs by extending innovation to 200 children in the community who don’t have access to formal education. On the occasion of ATL Community Day students of Ryan International School, Faridabad visited Govt. Primary School located in Gandhi Colony, Faridabad. The group of students of Atal Tinkering Lab of the school , Prasan Mittal, Rudra Mittal, P.Tarun Kumar, Vansh Bisht and Shrut presented and demonstrated many creative ideas as exhibits .

It comprised of working models and investigation based projects before the students of primary school. It included models like The cleaning bot, Smart street lights, automatic car cleaner , dustbin made with the concept of best out of waste and many more were displayed. An endeavour to promote scientific attitude and focus on creating more awareness of science and technology, this exhibit was one of it’s kind.Children of primary school with great zeal and enthusiasm enjoyed the exhibit and gathered ample ideas to create their own space. It not only polished their skills but was a step towards all round development.
