Atal Innovation Mission

PMO India
The true growth of a nation can be measured by scientific temperament and technological approach of the students. Children were invited from various schools and NGOs to have HANDS ON experience of Tinkering and Innovations. It was an endeavour to provide a common platform to various schools, teachers and students to exhibit their innovative ideas. The aim of the event was to Imagine, Invent and Inspire students. The activities successfully created an atmosphere of empathy and an environment where students as well as NGOs got a chance to share their ideas and innovations. Students also witnessed a Science Exhibition which was a stunning display of innovative projects and models based on various themes. Overall it was a knowledgeable and informative day for everyone. It provided a platform to budding scientists to express their talent and gain confidence. The experience was enriching for one and all. The event was also graced by the presence of Mr.Himanshu from NITI Aayog
